MAINTENANCE, CARE AND GOOD USE

Good use, care and maintenance of your earphones will extend its life.
Maintain that mint look

  • Wipe exteriors with a soft moist cloth (not wet) at a suitable frequency depending on your usage
  • Do not use chemical products or strong detergents to clean the earphones
  • Do not drop, subject to strong vibration or strike with hard objects to avoid damage to the electronic circuits
  • Put the device back in the case after use.
  • To maintain the health of the internal battery, do not charge for more than 10 hours at a stretch.

ENJOY RESPONSIBLY

  • Do not use wireless devices where it is strictly prohibited, such as while operating a vehicle, crossing a street, or during any activity where full awareness of your surroundings is required.
  • We’d recommend listening at minimum volume when using the device for calls and music so you can protect yourself from any damage to your ears.
  • We’d recommend not sharing your device with others (for better ear hygiene).
  • The device has a non-removable built-in battery which can only be replaced by our service center. Do not attempt to remove or replace it on your own.
  • Turn off the device while cleaning it.
